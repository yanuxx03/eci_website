Academic Programs and Senior High School Tracks

This page lists the academic programs and senior high school tracks available at Educare College, Inc. Students can easily view descriptions and career opportunities for each track to help them choose the program that matches their interests and future goals.

Tracks Offered

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

The STEM strand focuses on scientific knowledge, research, technology, and problem-solving skills.

Career Opportunities:

  • Engineer
  • Doctor
  • Scientist
  • Software Developer
  • Researcher

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management

The ABM strand develops skills in business operations, financial management, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

Career Opportunities:

  • Accountant
  • Business Owner
  • Marketing Specialist
  • Financial Analyst
  • Entrepreneur

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences

The HUMSS strand focuses on communication, social studies, culture, politics, and human behavior.

Career Opportunities:

  • Teacher
  • Lawyer
  • Journalist
  • Psychologist
  • Public Relations Officer

TVL

Technical Vocational Livelihood

The TVL strand provides practical skills and technical training for employment, entrepreneurship, and further studies.

Career Opportunities:

  • Technician
  • Chef
  • Computer Technician
  • Skilled Worker
  • Entrepreneur

Admissions Requirements

  • Good Moral Certificate
